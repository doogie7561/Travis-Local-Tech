About Travis Local Tech

Travis Local Tech provides simple, reliable technology help for individuals, families, and small businesses. The goal is to make technology less frustrating by offering practical troubleshooting, clear explanations, and straightforward service.

Services include website development, slow computer troubleshooting, Wi-Fi and printer support, device setup, software and application testing, file backup setup, password and account security, and other general technology needs.

Note: Some issues may require a hardware repair shop, internet provider, device replacement, or manufacturer support. I will explain that clearly if it comes up.
